Mesuic Bot - Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Mesuic Bot, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using the bot.

Data Collection

Mesuic Bot collects only the information necessary to perform its functions. We do not collect personal information such as names, addresses, or emails. The data we collect includes:

  • Music play history
  • Server settings (such as the music queue and volume)
  • Command usage and interaction logs

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely for improving the functionality of the bot and providing the best user experience. We do not share your data with third parties for any reason.

Data Retention

Mesuic Bot does not retain user data for long periods. Your interaction data (such as command usage and server settings) is stored temporarily for functionality purposes and is deleted after the session ends or after a reasonable period of inactivity.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this privacy policy at any time. If we make any changes, we will update this page accordingly. By continuing to use the bot after any changes, you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the data we collect, feel free to contact us through our official support channels.
