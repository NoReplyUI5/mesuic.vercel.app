Privacy Policy

Your privacy is crucial to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Mesuic Bot, the Discord music bot, collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. User Interactions

When you interact with Mesuic Bot, we may collect and process information such as your Discord user ID, username, and messages for the purpose of providing music playback services and improving user experience.

2. Music Playback

Mesuic Bot may store temporary data related to music playback, such as song titles, artists, and playlist information. This data is used solely for the purpose of delivering music services and is not permanently retained.

3. Bot Commands

Commands issued to Mesuic Bot, including but not limited to play, skip, and stop, may be logged for troubleshooting and improvement purposes. No sensitive user information is stored during this process.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Mesuic Bot may integrate with third-party services for music playback. Please review the privacy policies of these services, as we do not control their data practices.

5. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
